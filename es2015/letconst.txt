## **Quiz**

- What is the difference between ***var*** and ***let***?
Can reassign and redeclare with var and can reassign but not redeclare with let. 
Can access hoisted variable with var. let is block scope

- What is the difference between **var** and ***const***?
Can reassign and redeclare with var and cannot reassign and redeclare with const. 
Can access hoisted variable with var. const is block scope

- What is the difference between **let** and **const**?
Can reassign and cannot redeclare with let and cannot reassign and redeclare with const. 

- What is hoisting?
Variables are hoisted to the top of the scope that they are declared in.
var lets you access hoisted variables but they are undefined till they are used later on
